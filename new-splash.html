Bienvenue à FORT SAVANE

Qui sommes-nous?

Since 2013 this residence has been welcoming customers who have all appreciated the calm, comfort and exceptional discretion of our residence. In the city center of Fort de France, the Residence Fort Savane offers you an ideal accommodation solution during your business or personal trips. It is located on the very lively Rue de la Liberté, opposite the emblematic 18th century garden, La Savane. Close to the seafront, close to entertainment, shops, restaurants, all in a warm and comfortable setting. The ferry terminal is within walking distance. A taxi rank is present, just in front, too. This is where throughout your stay, my team and I will bring you attention, help and suggestions so that you live your experience in FORT-DE-FRANCE at 100% and so that it remains unforgettable. To welcome you with kindness and naturalness, to make sure that you feel good as soon as you walk through the front door: this is our deepest desire. I wish you an excellent stay with us in the heart of the city.
